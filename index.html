Our Story

Raising Poultry

Got Questions?

Frequently Asked

Do you deliver outside Lagos and Ogun State? +
Yes! We currently deliver to Lagos, Ogun, Oyo, and Osun states. For orders to other states, please contact us via WhatsApp or call our delivery hotline to get a custom quote based on your location and order size.
Are your chickens truly free-range and antibiotic-free? +
Absolutely. Our broilers are raised in open, ventilated coops with access to natural sunlight and pasture. We use zero growth hormones and no prophylactic antibiotics. Our feed is a certified organic blend inspected quarterly by licensed veterinary officers.
What is the minimum order quantity for day-old chicks? +
The minimum order for day-old chicks is 50 birds for local pickup and 100 birds for delivery. This ensures safe and stress-free transportation. For bulk orders of 500+ chicks, we offer discounted pricing — reach out to us for a wholesale quote.
How do I place a large or recurring order? +
For bulk or standing orders (restaurants, hotels, caterers), simply reach out via our WhatsApp checkout or contact form. We offer weekly and bi-weekly supply contracts with priority dispatch, dedicated account management, and negotiated pricing.
